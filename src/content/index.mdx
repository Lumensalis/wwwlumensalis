## Overview

Lumensalis is focused on helping makers and hobbyists add interactive 
effects to their projects without having to spend years learning to program. 
We recently released the [Lumenalis CircuitPython Framework](Projects/LCPF/LCPF.mdx), 
which aims to help hobbyists make their creations shake, sing, and shine.
